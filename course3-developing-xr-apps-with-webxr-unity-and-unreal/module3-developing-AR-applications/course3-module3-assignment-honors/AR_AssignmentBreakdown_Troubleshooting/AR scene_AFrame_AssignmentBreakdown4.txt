Phase 4: Combining Camera Orbit and Animation

To merge the best of both scenes, I took the following steps:

Kept the camera rig structure from viewerstyle with orbit-controls attached

Used the lighting and plane configuration from vstyle for better ambient results

Ensured that the <a-entity> loading the .glb model used the animation-mixer component

Adjusted position, scale, and rotation to center the Dino close to the camera but not too zoomed in

Applied scale="0.5 0.5 0.5" and position="0 1.5 -2.5" for optimal layout

Final tweaks ensured the scene launched correctly under localhost:8000/viewerstyle.html.
