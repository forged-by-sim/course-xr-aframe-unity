Development Approach and Tools 

I used Blender for rigging and animating the Rhomaleosaurus model, and initially attempted to deploy it in Unity for AR. Due to limitations with mobile playback, I pivoted to A-Frame (WebXR) for the VR viewer and MyWebAR for the final AR experience.

My goal was to implement a looped swimming animation and allow users to physically walk around the model using marker-less AR. The biggest challenge was achieving cross-platform compatibility while preserving animation playback.

In the end, I was able to fully realize my vision of a lightweight, immersive XR scene accessible on both desktop and mobile browsers.
