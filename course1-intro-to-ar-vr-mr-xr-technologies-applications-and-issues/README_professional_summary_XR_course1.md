## üìç Overview

This course laid the **conceptual and observational foundation** for understanding the XR ecosystem. It introduced key distinctions between **Augmented Reality (AR)**, **Virtual Reality (VR)**, and **Mixed Reality (MR)**, while exploring how they all fall under the broader umbrella of **Extended Reality (XR)**.

Through a series of visual critiques and real-world XR app explorations, I began developing a critical lens toward XR platforms, their use-cases, and their impact on society. This course shaped my trajectory as a simulation technologist, helping me identify ethical challenges, meaningful applications, and technical affordances across platforms.

Each module below includes a small but focused hands-on assignment ‚Äî using apps like **Seek by iNaturalist** and **Complete Anatomy** to understand XR through direct observation.
